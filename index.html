about_me

I am an AEM Developer and UI designer with 11 years of experience creating impactful digital experiences, currently specializing in AI-powered solutions.

Currently, I work as an AEM Developer at LeapPoint, where I leverage Adobe Experience Manager and Artificial Intelligence to build next-generation web experiences. My expertise spans the intersection of traditional development and cutting-edge AI automation.

My recent experience includes roles as Sr. Web Developer at REVOLT, Frontend Developer at Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts (designing homepage components with React.js), and Lead Content Author for Adobe Experience Manager at AB InBev (Anheuser-Busch), where I spent over 3 years mastering AEM development and content strategy.

I've worked with industry-leading companies including LeapPoint, REVOLT, Lincoln Center, AB InBev, New Balance Inc., Wells Fargo, and Adobe. I am proficient in Adobe Experience Manager (AEM), React.js, Node.js, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and the full Adobe Creative Suite.

Beyond client work, I founded Altared Alchemie, a faith-driven AI consultancy where I help businesses, churches, and creators leverage AI to automate workflows, scale operations, and create intelligent digital solutions including chatbots, AI avatars, and custom automation systems.
